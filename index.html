Australia is home to over 800 bird species

Australia is home to 800 species of some of the world's most extraordinary birds, with 370 of those found nowhere else on earth. Yet many of these avian wonders are teetering on the brink of extinction.

20% of Australia is inhabited

It's more accurate to say that a significant portion of Australia's population lives in the coastal areas, with a large portion of the land being desert or wilderness.

Every kid goes through a Wildlife Warrior Program

While not a formal program that every Australian child attends, the concept of "Wildlife Warriors," championed by the Irwin family, encourages young people to become advocates for wildlife and conservation through initiatives like the Visionary Wildlife Warriors program, and events like the Wildlife Warriors Readathon.
